About Assured Vending Group

Independent Operators.
National Strength.

Founded in 2025, Assured Vending Group is a trusted UK network of independent vending machine operators providing nationwide vending and refreshment solutions for businesses across the country.

Our consortium brings together experienced local vending companies to deliver reliable service, competitive pricing, and high-quality solutions backed by national coverage. By working collaboratively, our members benefit from shared expertise, stronger purchasing power, and preferred supplier partnerships.

Unlike a franchise, every Assured Vending Group member remains independently owned and operated, combining personalised local service with the strength and scale of a national vending network.
